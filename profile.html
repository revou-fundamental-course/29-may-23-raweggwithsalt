About Us


The Spider Society is a form of charity where we accept donation to help people that cannot afford daily needs, such as food, water, or even educational needs. With the existence of Spider Society, we hope that we can help those that are in need. The concept of Spider Society is taken by the words said by Mary Jane on the animated movie Spiderman : Into the Spiderverse where she mentioned about being a hero doesn't mean being Spiderman, hero can be anyone, even when we don't have a mask of a hero, we still can act like a hero to help those around us. Through this concept, we invite all of you to join the Spider Society and become the hero to help people.
